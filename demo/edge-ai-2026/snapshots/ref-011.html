Waymo 6th Generation: Moving 95% of Inference to the Edge

Waymo Research • February 2026 • Technical Report

Abstract

In this technical report, we describe how our sixth-generation autonomous driving system processes 95% of inference workloads on the vehicle's edge computing units, with only 5% of critical decisions requiring cloud support. This represents a fundamental shift from our previous architecture, which relied on cloud computing for approximately 40% of inference tasks.

Key Achievement: 95% on-vehicle inference with 99.99% availability, achieving an average decision latency of 4.2ms — well within the 10ms safety threshold.

Performance Metrics

  • Decision latency: 4.2ms average (vs. 12ms in 5th gen)
  • Compute efficiency: 3.2 TOPS/Watt
  • Redundancy: Triple-redundant edge compute units
  • Cloud dependency: Reduced from 40% to 5%
