Gartner Predicts 60% of Enterprise AI Will Run at the Edge by 2028

Gartner Research • February 2026 • Technology Forecast

The shift toward edge computing is accelerating faster than previously anticipated. Our latest forecast shows that By 2028, more than 60% of enterprise AI workloads will be deployed at the edge, up from less than 15% in 2024.

This dramatic shift is being driven by several converging factors:

  • Latency requirements — Real-time applications like autonomous vehicles and industrial robotics demand sub-10ms inference times
  • Data sovereignty — Increasingly strict regulations require data to be processed locally
  • Cost optimization — Edge processing reduces cloud data transfer costs by up to 70%
  • Model efficiency — Advances in model compression enable deployment of sophisticated AI models on edge hardware

"Organizations that fail to develop an edge AI strategy by 2027 risk being left behind as their competitors unlock real-time intelligence at the point of action."
— Dr. Sarah Chen, VP Analyst, Gartner

Key Recommendations

Gartner recommends that IT leaders:

  1. Assess current AI workloads for edge migration potential
  2. Invest in edge-optimized hardware platforms
  3. Develop standardized model deployment pipelines
  4. Implement federated learning for privacy-preserving model updates
